Our Vision

Raising Kings &
Priests in the
Marketplace

This program is dedicated to imparting the exact knowledge required for career and business excellence - raising balanced professionals who understand their place in the marketplace and are equipped to excel in all areas.

Expect rapid career growth, transformation of existing businesses, and the creation of new ventures solving real-world problems.

Mentorship, excellence, and vision - the three pillars of marketplace dominion.

- The SODE Mandate

Our Objectives

  • Raise a community of successful and Impact-Conscious christian career and Business professionals through trainings.
  • Encourage the christian community to strive to take our position at the top ("you shall be above only").
  • Thy Kingdom Come: Awaken consciousness of christian career and business professionals to the enormous responsibility ahead of us as it relates enforcing and establishing God's kingdom agenda.
  • Raising Christian Leaders: Raise christians who will lead at various levels within the career and business world.
  • Provide mentorship and guidance to aspiring christian career and Business professionals.
  • Raise a christian support group community - Iron sharpens Iron.
  • Leverage lessons from Daniel, Joseph, and Esther as the core source of truth throughout the training.

Who Should Attend

Every christian or individual that intends to attain success in business and career through scriptural principles.
